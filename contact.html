Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent technical issues, we aim to respond within 4-6 hours during business days.

Do you offer technical support for your tools?

Yes! We provide comprehensive technical support for all our tools. Whether you're facing issues or need guidance, our team is here to help.

Can I suggest new features for your tools?

Absolutely! We love hearing feature suggestions from our users. Many of our best features started as user suggestions.

Are your tools really free?

Yes, all our basic tools are completely free to use. We believe in making quality educational and professional tools accessible to everyone.

Do you offer custom solutions for organizations?

Yes, we provide custom solutions and enterprise packages for educational institutions and businesses. Contact us for more information.

How can I report a bug?

You can report bugs through our contact form, feedback system, or by emailing support@taskmate.com. Please include detailed steps to reproduce the issue.
