üí° Professional Tips for Best Results

Keep it Concise

Limit your CV to 2 pages maximum. Recruiters spend only 6-8 seconds on initial screening.

Use Action Verbs

Start bullet points with words like "Managed," "Developed," "Increased," "Implemented."

Tailor for Each Job

Customize your CV for specific job applications by highlighting relevant skills and experiences.

Quantify Achievements

Use numbers to show impact: "Increased sales by 25%," "Managed team of 10 people."
